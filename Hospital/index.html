About Us

Founded in 1997 by a team of India’s leading cardiologists, CARE Hospitals started its journey as a 100-bedded Heart Institute with a core team of 20 cardiologists, 1 operating theatre, and 1 catheterization laboratory. 25 years later, the CARE Hospitals Group is a multi-speciality healthcare provider with 17 healthcare facilities serving 7 cities across 6 states in India.

It is the regional leader in South and Central India and is amongst the top 5 Pan-Indian hospital chains. Darkside Hospitals delivers comprehensive care in over 30 specialities.
